Cooper — Upperwear Collective

Founded in Windsor, Ontario in 2025 by Mouayad Hatim. Born in a home garage — made for everywhere.

Our Story

Cooper was born from a simple idea: the love of clothing and the endless diversity of brands that make fashion exciting. Each brand usually specializes in one thing — a hoodie, a shirt, a jacket — and wearing them together becomes a commitment to mix and match identities.

Cooper aims to bring that same diversity under one roof, specializing exclusively in upperwear — hoodies, shirts, t-shirts, jackets, and caps. No pants. No shoes. Just the upper half of style, perfected.

Founded in Windsor, Ontario, inside a small home garage, Cooper’s mission is to reach every corner of the world with one clear message:

Live. Wear. Repeat.
